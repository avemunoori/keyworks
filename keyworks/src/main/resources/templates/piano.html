Help

Getting Started

  1. Select a MIDI device from the dropdown
  2. Click "Connect" to connect to the device
  3. Click "Start Recording" to begin capturing MIDI notes
  4. Play your MIDI instrument
  5. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  6. Click "View PDF" to preview the sheet music
  7. Click "Download PDF" to save the sheet music to your computer

Virtual Keyboard

You can use the virtual keyboard to test MIDI input without a physical device:

  • Click on keys to play notes
  • Start recording first to capture the notes

Click "Simulate Input" to generate a C major scale for testing.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with PDF generation:

  • Make sure LilyPond is installed on your system
  • Check the LilyPond log tab for any error messages
  • Verify that the output directory is writable
  • Try the "Test PDF Generation" button below to verify your setup
